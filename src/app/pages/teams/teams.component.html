The following section details the requirements for all teams participation in rankings

All teams are ranked within their respective servers. i.e. Teams in the EU servers are ranked in EU rankings.

Teams must participate in five (5) Ranked events within a two-week time period to qualify for team rankings.

Host ranking status will reset if no events are held within 3 weeks.

Ranking Time Period per month:

  • First Ranking = 1st - 15th
  • Second Ranking Week = 16th - end of Month
